About Our Chapter

The TSA chapter at Ravenwood High School gives us an opportunity to participate in engaging STEM activities while having fun. We’re so grateful to have a chapter that allows us to explore our interests and collaborate with other students who share a love for technology and innovation!

Why It's Fun and Rewarding!

Being a part of the TSA chapter is about more than competitions; it’s a rewarding experience filled with laughter, teamwork, friendships, and growth. We participate in exciting projects, learn new skills, and make long-lasting friendships along the way. Each step we take with this club enables us to grow and share our passion for STEM.
