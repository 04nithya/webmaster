We partner with local farms and collect natural groceries from them once a month to make our food. This ensures a healthy, vegan experience for all our guests, an experience that they cherish.

Join Us for Our Monthly Workshop!

At Saffron Spice Workshop, we believe in the importance of sourcing our food from local farms. Every month, we put on a free workshop where you can grab some fresh produce from our top local farmers and learn how to cook them. Come hang out with us for some tips on growing your own veggies and using them in your favorite dishes. Together, we can celebrate the flavors of local harvests and connect with the community!

When, Where, and How to Join?

The first 10 customers to come to Saffron Spice located at 0416 BestRestaurant Lane 11625, TN at 10AM every third Sunday of the month get to join us!
